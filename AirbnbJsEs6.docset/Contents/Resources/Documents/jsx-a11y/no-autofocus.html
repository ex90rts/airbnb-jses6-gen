no-autofocus

Enforce that autoFocus prop is not used on elements. Autofocusing elements can cause usability issues for sighted and non-sighted users, alike.

References

  1. w3c

Rule details

This rule takes no arguments.

Succeed

<div />

Fail

<div autoFocus />
<div autoFocus="true" />
<div autoFocus="false" />
<div autoFocus={undefined} />
